## Role Vision

Transform a legacy educational institution into a digitally compelling and emotionally resonant brand for parents and students in a competitive school landscape.

## What I Did

Designed a multi-platform content and media strategy to unify the digital tone across branches

Directed and produced story-driven video content highlighting student experience, achievements, culture & community

Partnered with academic & creative teams to ensure brand consistency and narrative depth

Built data-led audience personas (parents, educators, alumni, aspirants) and tailored communication accordingly

Implemented engagement funnels to convert interest → enquiry → admission

Introduced structured performance reviews using Meta Insights & GA4 to optimize content frequency, tone & ad strategy

## Key Highlights

Shifted brand tone from academic-only to human-centered storytelling

Elevated digital trust & parent engagement

Increased visibility for flagship programs, events & initiatives

Streamlined content execution across teams
