## Role Vision

Build high-intent lead ecosystems for residential and commercial projects through authority-driven and value-based marketing.

## What I Did

Designed full-funnel real estate marketing strategy (Discovery → Consideration → Conversion)

Built brand identity & storytelling themes aligned to trust, lifestyle & investment value

Directed social media content, ad campaigns, landing pages & builder credibility narratives

Ran lead-generation performance campaigns via Meta & Google

Established buyer personas (NRI investors, relocating families, premium home buyers, first-time buyers)

Audited website UX & communication tone; implemented SEO fundamentals & schema-based content

## Key Highlights

Generated consistent qualified leads with reduced cost per lead (CPL)

Strengthened digital brand credibility through proof-based content (project milestones, testimonials, walkthroughs)

Introduced structured nurturing via retargeting & content sequencing
